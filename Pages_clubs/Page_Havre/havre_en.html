The Le Havre club was founded in 1894. According to the club, it was created in 1872 but this has been proven to be inaccurate. On October 20, 1884, Pastor Jeffery Edward Orlebar, with some former members of FC Havrais, created a new club under the name Havre Athletic Club. No known source clearly mentions the playing of footbal within the club between 1884 and 1894, although it is possible that football matches within the club between members. In the absence of other elements, only the date of 1894 can therefore be retained as the date of founding of the football team. Its colors are sky blue and navy blue.
