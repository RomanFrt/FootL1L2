Main page

The Reims club was founded in 1931. In 1935, the club opted for professionalism and joined the second division of the French championship. In the 1950s, Stade de Reims established itself as the leading French club, amassing a prestigious track record in just a few years. The satde of Reims returns to the honorary division of the North-East, 6th level of French football, from where it will take ten seasons to move up to Ligue 2. 33 years after its last season at the highest national level, Reims finally makes his return to Ligue 2.
