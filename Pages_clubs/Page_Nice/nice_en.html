The Nice club was founded in 1904. The players are traditionally called "Les Aiglons". The original colors are blue and black and will later become black and red. From 1927 to 1929, the club played in Promotion d'Honneur Sud-Est, then in the Honorary Division from 1929 to 1932. The fifties were the most glorious for OGC Nice with four French championship titles in eight seasons. . The Ray stadium, an old stadium, was packed and spectators were sometimes seated on the edge of the pitch.
